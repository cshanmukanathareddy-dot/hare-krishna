IoT Basics

Learn how Internet of Things connects devices and powers smart systems.

Introduction

The Internet of Things (IoT) is a technology that connects devices to the internet, enabling them to communicate and share data. It is widely used in smart homes, healthcare, agriculture, and industries.

Key Concepts

IoT includes sensors, connectivity, cloud processing, and user interfaces. These components work together to collect and analyze data.

Applications

IoT is used in smart homes, healthcare monitoring, industrial automation, and smart cities.

Advantages

It improves efficiency, enables automation, and provides real-time insights.

Conclusion

IoT is shaping the future by connecting devices and enabling intelligent systems.
